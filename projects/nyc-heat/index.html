rent stabilization and heating complaints in NYC

dec 2015

This is an interactive map of New York City, visualizing rent stabilization, number of 311 heating complaints, and demographic data by zip code.

about

Created at the 2015 Hack for Heat code sprint, hosted by ODSC and betaNYC. Winner of the "Best Map" award, sponsored by CartoDB. Data from here and here. Collaborators: Lauren Long, Ilona Brand, Max McCrea, Shad Hopson, Melanie Freed.
