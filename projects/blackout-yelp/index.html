Blackout Yelp Poetry

6. March 2016

This is a collection of blackout poetry based on one person’s Yelp reviews. The subject I’ve chosen is a suburban mom from the East Bay (of California) who mostly reviews place around San Francisco and the East Bay, but has clusters of reviews in other cities where she goes to vacation, like Portland and Las Vegas. She likes brunch, sex toys, and strip clubs.
