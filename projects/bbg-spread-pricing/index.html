how middlemen make money from drugs

sept 2018

This is an investigative feature piece detailing the complicated process in the world of pharmaceutical companies known as spread pricing, where companies like CVS mark up the difference between what they reimburse pharmacies for a drug and the amount they charge their clients. I created an interactive explanatory graphic that walks the reader through how spread pricing works and data visualizations showing the dramatic spreads of certain popular drugs.
