here’s who actually votes in american elections

BLOOMBERG, NOV 2018

This piece examines who votes on behalf of whom in American elections, comparing the demographic breakdown of the current United States population to who voted in the 2016 and 2014 elections. I helped create a series of graphics that represent various populations broken down by age, race and voter status, and how voter suppression laws and other voting restrictions prevent large swathes of the young, non-white population from voting.
