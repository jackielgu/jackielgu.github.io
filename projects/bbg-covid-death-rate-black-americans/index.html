majority black counties see triple the covid death rate

may 2020

This project illustrates the Covid-19 death rate in every U.S. county over time, reveailng how much faster the Covid-19 death rate rose among majority-Black counties. I analyzed daily deaths data from JHU to design and develop the graphics. This story was updated two months later in July 2020, to see if the trajectory of the data had changed. (It hadn't.)
