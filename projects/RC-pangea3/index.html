fossils on ancient maps

nov 2015

This is an interactive map visualizing fossil paleogeography and continental drift over a timeline of 230 million years. I made this in a team with four other programmers at Hack the Dinos 2015, a paleontology-themed open-data hackathon hosted at the American Museum of Natural History.

Click the image to check it out.
